OUR HERITAGE

In Cambodian culture, an Apsara is a beautiful supernatural being who performs the “Apsara Dance” exclusively for the King of Cambodia.

While attending night school, Mrs. Thao Te noticed many in the community were too busy to cook authentic Asian meals. For a small fee, she began preparing home-cooked dinners, attracting curious passersby eager to try her food. This demand led her to open Apsara Asian Restaurant, where she initially served whatever she had prepared that day. Through word of mouth, it became known for its delicious, authentic cuisine and generous portions.
